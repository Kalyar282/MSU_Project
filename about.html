About Us

Our Mission

The Mysterious Species of Underwater (MSU) website aims to develop a new and ambitious vision for underwater species that exist and news occuring over the whole world. A vision embracing the spirit of sustainability, well-being and equity for humankind as its guiding principle. A vision that is in harmony with the ocean, a vision that will lead to the development of a MSU.

What Makes MSU Different?

The MSU website have developed by one of the ALPHA Group members Kalyar Myo Nyunt.
